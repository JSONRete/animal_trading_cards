Lion

Opposition can hear nightly roars of primarily male lions up to five miles away throughout a territory done to exhibit a presence.

  • Binomial Nomenclature: Panthera leo
  • Wild Population: 20,000
  • Average Lifespan:10-15 Years
  • Habitat: sub-Saharan Africa (mainly)

Lions have a recognized stocky build in comparison to most wild cat species. With powerful forelegs, teeth, and jaws, the king of the jungle dominates and kills its prey. Most genetics of the species produce cats with coats of a yellow-gold with adult males growing manes with colors varying from blond to reddish-brown to black..
